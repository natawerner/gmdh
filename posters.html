Collecting Fists

These posters are comprised of four layers each screen printed in a different type of black ink. Each layer is a fist image out of one of the categories into which I sorted the original collection of images. The categories are based on the date and the geographical location of their use/publication in relation to the time and place of my making the project in Moscow in 2020.

The posters explore how the use of the symbol changed and evolved over time and reflect on the current connotation that it carries. The series is an attempt to clarify the symbol’s meaning after it has been distorted and diluted over more than a century, or rather an attempt to reflect on this uncertainty and find peace with it.
